Welcome to my online portfolio. I am Steve Bickram, a Canadian Graphic Designer, illustrator, coder, logo designer, and photographer. Please feel free to read about me and view some of my work bellow. I am looking to make new contacts for internship opportunities and to connect with like minded artists. Please do not hesitate to contact me through my posted information.

I've completed a degree in Economics and have a diverse background in business and design. As an entrepreneur my problem solving skills, creativity, communication skills, and most of all, my work ethic have all helped me to start and run a succesful home cleaning service. These same qualities have helped me achieve goals outside of the business enviroment. As a passionate martial artist I have been learning and absorbing different forms of martial arts over the last fourteen years and teach kickboxing classes at Algonquin college. I take pride in my commitment to my passions in all aspects of my life.

From I young age I always had an appreciation for the arts. Everything from film and music, to dance and style has captivated me. This has led me down different paths creatively with a broad range of experiences. I've had the opportunities to expirement with cosplay, poster design, t-shirt design, illustration, and scultping all before deciding to pursue graphic design as a career. These experiences have helped to develop my creativity and visual skills, and have turned molded me into the designer I am today.
