Illustrations

Illustrating was my first creative practice. Throughout grade school and forever afterwards, there was always more illustrations in my notebooks than there were actual notes. As a means of increasing my digital illustration skills, during the summer of 2015 I undertook a project where I illustrated 100 digital illustrations in 100 days. The images on this page are a collection of some of those illustrations.
