Illustration

Illustration is a love I discovered early on in life and has alway been with me. As a means of increasing my digital illustration skills, during the summer of 2015, I undertook a project where I illustrated 100 digital illustrations in 100 days. I set out to challenge myself to get out of my comfort zone in many ways during this project and took it as a time to develop different styles of illustration. I feel it is important to exit my comfort zone and try new things in order to force myself away from stagnation. The images on this page are a collection of some of those illustrations.
