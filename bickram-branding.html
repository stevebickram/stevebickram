Branding

Off The Dome is a hat manufacturer that I helped develop with two fellow students from the Algonquin College Graphic Design program. Together we branded the business, sold both online and in person, and campaigned a kickstarter for equipment funding which had great success. During the project I learned about product design, branding, sales, and made great friends and connections. It was truly a great experience.
