Off The Dome Branding

Off The Dome is a millinary that I helped develop with two fellow students in the Algonquin College Graphic Design program. Together we branded the business, sold both online and in person, and campaigned a kickstarter for equipment funding. During the project I learned alot about product design, branding, sales, and made some really great friends. It was truly a great experience.
